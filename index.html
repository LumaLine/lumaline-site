Welcome to LumaLine

LumaLine is an innovative graduation project that tackles one of the most pressing challenges of our era — sustainable transportation. By harnessing the power of solar energy, LumaLine reimagines how trains can operate cleanly, efficiently, and independently from fossil fuels.

Our mission directly aligns with the goals of Saudi Vision 2030 and the Saudi Green Initiative, both of which aim to reduce carbon emissions, diversify the economy, and transition toward clean energy. LumaLine offers a realistic, scalable solution that could drastically reshape the future of transport in Saudi Arabia and beyond.
