Welcome to LumaLine

LumaLine is a solar-powered train initiative that supports the goals of Saudi Vision 2030 and the Saudi Green Initiative. Our goal is to revolutionize transportation by replacing fossil fuels with clean solar energy—cutting emissions, reducing costs, and pushing Saudi Arabia into a sustainable, high-tech future.
