Welcome to LumaLine

LumaLine is a visionary graduation project dedicated to redefining the future of sustainable transportation in line with Saudi Vision 2030 and the Saudi Green Initiative. By leveraging solar energy to power high-speed rail, we present a bold, scalable solution to reduce emissions and revolutionize mobility across the Kingdom.
